## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function, remember preferences, or (where used) measure usage.

## How we use cookies

**Strictly necessary.** Some cookies are essential for the website to work, for example to remember a choice you make on this site (such as dismissing a notice).

**Analytics.** We may use privacy-conscious or first-party analytics to understand aggregate traffic and improve the site. If we enable third-party analytics cookies, we will describe them here and, where required by law, ask for your consent before they are set.

## Similar technologies

We may use local storage in your browser for the same purposes as strictly necessary cookies—for example to remember that you acknowledged a notice so it is not shown on every visit.

## Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking strictly necessary cookies may affect how parts of the site work.
