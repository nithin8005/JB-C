## Who we are

This website is operated by JBasani&Company (“**we**”, “**us**”, or “**our**”). This policy describes how we handle personal information collected through this site.

## Information we may collect

When you contact us or submit a form, we may receive your name, email address, company name, and message content. We may also collect technical data sent by your browser, such as IP address, device type, browser version, and pages visited, where needed to operate and secure the site.

## How we use information

We use this information to respond to enquiries, improve the website, maintain security, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal information.

## Legal bases (where applicable)

Depending on your location, we may rely on consent, legitimate interests (for example operating and securing the website), or performance of a contract when processing personal data.

## Retention

We retain information only for as long as needed for the purposes above, unless a longer period is required by law.

## Sharing

We may share information with service providers who assist us in hosting, email, or security, under appropriate confidentiality and data-processing terms. We may also disclose information if required by law.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures designed to protect personal information. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

## Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details on our [Contact](/contact) page.

## International transfers

If you access this site from outside the country where we operate, your information may be processed in countries that may not provide the same level of data protection as your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “last updated” indication at the bottom of this page will be revised when material changes are made.

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the contact details on our [Contact](/contact) page.
